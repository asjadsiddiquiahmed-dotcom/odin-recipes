Pancakes

Description

A fluffy delicious traditional milk, egg, flour based pancake. This is a delicious brown crispy pancake recipe. It is very simple only including a couple ingredients that are very easily accesible.

The pancake recipe is also very easy to follow, easy to remember, and very easy to quickly execute in a short amount of time.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Mix 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour with 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder.
  2. Mix 1 tablespoon white sugar with ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste.
  3. Mix 1 ¼ cups milk with 3 tablespoons butter, melted with 1 large egg.
  4. Mix all your ingredients into one bowl.
  5. Heat up pan up to medium heat.
  6. Add either oil or butter to create non-stick effect.
  7. Use 1/4 cup to scoop batter and pour batter into pan, each scoop being a pancake.
